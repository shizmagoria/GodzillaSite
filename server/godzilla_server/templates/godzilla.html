Godzilla

Godzilla (ゴジラ Gojira), also dubbed Titanus Gojira, is a giant reptilian daikaiju created by Legendary Pictures and (alongside Kong) the titular main kaiju protagonist of the MonsterVerse franchise, first appearing in the 2014 film, Godzilla.
